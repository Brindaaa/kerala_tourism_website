Welcome to Kozhikode

Kozhikode as an important travel destination in Kerala offers a number of experiences to relish and several must see places.
Amongst the prime experiences is the visit to Iringa Crafts Village. Here artisans and craftsmen are encouraged to show their skills and sell their products to visiting tourists.
An experience worth savouring in Kozhikode is its scrumptious delicacy, of which the 'Kozhikodian Halwa' is of prime importance. Kozhikode, erstwhile Calicut is known as the city of spices. So, don't forget to purchase spices including pepper, cinnamon, cloves and cardamom from here.
Kozhikode is also known for its deicious food, with all kinds of varieties, in non-vegetarian, vegetarian, and sea food as well.
